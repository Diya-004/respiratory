AI-assisted breathing sound analysis for early respiratory screening.

Upload a breathing sound captured from a digital stethoscope, smartphone, or low-cost device and get a condition estimate, severity flag, and a clinician-friendly report.

System Flow

1. Upload Capture from stethoscope, smartphone, or Raspberry Pi.
2. Feature Extraction MFCC, chroma, spectral, and temporal features summarize lung acoustics.
3. Inference Current MVP uses a swappable baseline that can later be replaced by CNN or CNN-LSTM models.
4. Clinical Output Condition label, severity level, urgent alert, explainability regions, and report.
